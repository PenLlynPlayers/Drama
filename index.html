About Us

A community where creativity shines and stories come to life.

The Dramatic Society was founded in 1985 with a simple mission: to bring quality, accessible theatre to our local community. We are a non-profit, volunteer-run organization dedicated to fostering a love for the performing arts. Our group was founded by our esteemed director, **Roberta**, a professional with many accolades to her name. We are also proud to be supported by the charity **AgeWell**, who help us in our mission. Whether you're a seasoned actor, a budding director, or someone who just loves to help behind the scenes with costumes or lighting, we have a place for you. We produce a range of plays, from classic dramas to modern comedies, and we believe that every member, on or off the stage, contributes to the magic of live performance.
